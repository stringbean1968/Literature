\chapter{Selected Legislation and Guidance References}
\label{app:Selected Legislation and Guidance References}

\begin{itemize}
  \item Veterinary Surgeons Act 1966.
  \item RCVS Code of Professional Conduct and supporting guidance.
  \item RCVS Practice Standards Scheme standards and modules.
  \item Animal Welfare Act 2006; Animal Health and Welfare (Scotland) Act 2006; Welfare of Animals Act (Northern Ireland) 2011.
  \item Veterinary Medicines Regulations 2013 and VMD guidance notes.
  \item Health and Safety at Work etc. Act 1974.
  \item Management of Health and Safety at Work Regulations 1999.
  \item Control of Substances Hazardous to Health Regulations 2002.
  \item Ionising Radiations Regulations 2017.
  \item Reporting of Injuries, Diseases and Dangerous Occurrences Regulations 2013.
  \item Misuse of Drugs Act 1971 and Misuse of Drugs Regulations 2001.
  \item Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005 and Fire (Scotland) Act 2005 framework.
  \item Hazardous Waste regulations and waste duty-of-care requirements.
  \item Animal By-Products legislation and implementation guidance.
  \item PUWER 1998; LOLER 1998; Pressure Systems Safety Regulations 2000.
  \item UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018.
  \item APHA/DEFRA guidance on notifiable diseases and biosecurity.
  \item BSAVA and AVA guidance relevant to peri-anaesthetic and clinical governance.
  \item RCVS Mind Matters Initiative and Vetlife wellbeing resources.
\end{itemize}
